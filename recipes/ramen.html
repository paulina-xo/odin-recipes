Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork, nori, menma, and scallions. Ramen has its roots in Chinese noodle dishes and is a part of Japanese Chinese cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Broth (chicken or veggie)
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Toppings: panko, egg, chili oil, etc...

Steps

  1. Stir-Fry Aromatics: Garlic and ginger, what a delicious duo. This is where the flavor is, friends.
  2. Make Your (Easy!) Broth: Add some chicken broth and dried shiitake mushrooms for some umami punch.
  3. Add Noodles: Cook your noodles right in the broth with some scallions (more flavor, please!).
  4. Add Veg: Thinly sliced kale, shredded carrots, whatever youâ€™d like! Cook until just tender.
  5. Top It Off: Add some crunchy panko crumbs, a soft-boiled egg, chili oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, and/or soy sauce, whatever your heart desires.
